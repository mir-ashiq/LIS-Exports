Royal Rajasthan

OVERVIEW :

Rajasthan, the land of kings, evokes a vibrant tapestry of colors, culture, and heritage. Nestled in the heart of India's Thar Desert, this enchanting state is a treasure trove of majestic forts, opulent palaces, and bustling bazaars.

ITINERARY :

  • DAY 1 : HOWRAH - JAIPUR

    Our representative will meet at sealdah station and will start our journey by Train from Sealdah 22.55 (Sealdah Ajmer), night at train.

  • DAY 2 JAIPUR

      Reach at Jaipur at 11.55 p.m., check in hotel, night stay at Jaipur

  • DAY 3 - JAIPUR SIGHTSEEING

    After breakfast visit the busy Bazaars, the Hawa mahal, and Jantar Mantar, City Palace. After lunch we visit Amber Fort & Palace, Jagat Shiromani Temple and pass through the scenic Jal Mahal. Overnight at Jaipur hotel.

  • DAY 4 JAIPUR SIGHTSEEING

    After breakfast visit City Palace. Nahargarh Fort, Evening visit local market (at your own cost), Overnight at Jaipur hotel

  • DAY 5 JAIPUR – PUSHKAR / AJMER

    After Breakfast we will start journey for Ajmer . After lunch we will Visit , Ajmer Sharif Dargah. Evening Time take Depart Pushkar .Visit brahma Temple, Pushkar Sarowar .Overnight at hotel at Pushkar.

  • DAY 6 PUSHKAR / AJMER - BIKANER

    After breakfast Check-Out Hotel & Depart for Bikaner, After lunch We Visit , Museums,Hawlei and temples of Bikaner. Overnight at Bikaner

  • DAY 7 BIKANER - JAISALMER

    After breakfast, drive to Jaisalmer. On the way Visit Kuldhara Village in the evening visit Sam sand Dunes & also enjoy Camel Safari (own cost)& Culture Programe in the camp. Overnight at dessert camp

  • Day 8 – : JAISALMER SIGHTSEEING

    After breakfast check out from the camp. After arriving Jaisalmer city &Check-in at hotel. We will visit Jaisalmer fort (SONAR KELLA), Bada Bagh, Patwaon ki Haveli, temples and Gadsisar Lake. Overnight Stay In Jaisalmer.

  • Day 9 – : JAISALMER - JODHPUR

    Check-Out Hotel & Depart Jaisalmer. Check-In Hotel at Jodhpur, visit the Mehrangarh Fort, situated on a low sandstone Hill, including Moti-Mahal and Phool Mahal. Also visit Jaswant Thada, an imposing Marble Memoriam built in 1899 and the museum located in the Umaid Public Gardens. Overnight at Jodhpur hotel.

  • Day 10–: Day 10 : JODHPUR - UDAIPUR

    After breakfast transfer to Udaipur Upon arrival check in at hotel. By the sunset we take boat ride over Lake Pichola (Own Cost) experience of which is beyond any comparison. Overnight at Udaipur.

  • Day 11 – : UDAIPUR SIGHTSEEING

    After Breakfst, for the day we visit the City Palace, Bagore ki Haveli, Saheliyon Ki Bari, Jagdish Temple, Pratap Smarak, Fateh Sagar Lake, Bhartiya Lok Kala Mandal & Shilp Gram. Overnight at Udaipur.

  • Day 12 – : UDAIPUR – CHITTORGARH - JODHPUR

    After Breakfast Check-Out Hotel. transfer to Jodhpur Railway station en route sightseen Chittorgarh, Drop Jodhpur Railway Station, train for Howrah at 23.55 p.m.

  • Day 13 – : TRAIN

    Full day in train. We will provide light food in train

  • Day 13 – : HOWRAH

    Reach at Howrah at 04.55 a.m with lots of sweet memories
