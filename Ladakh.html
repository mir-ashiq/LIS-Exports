OVERVIEW :

Discover the rugged beauty of Ladakh, a high-altitude desert in the Indian Himalayas. Known for its stunning landscapes, Buddhist monasteries, and unique culture, Ladakh offers an unforgettable experience. Explore this remote region with our 4 nights and 5 days package, perfect for adventure seekers and nature lovers.

ITINERARY :

  • DAY 1: Arrival in Srinagar

    Arrive at Leh Airport and transfer to your hotel. Check-in at the hotel and enjoy a leisurely evening exploring the local market. Overnight stay at the hotel.

  • DAY 2: Ladakh Sightseeing

    After breakfast, visit the famous Shanti Stupa and Leh Palace. Enjoy panoramic views of Leh and the surrounding mountains. In the evening, explore the local market. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

  • DAY 3: Adventure Activities

    Spend the day indulging in various adventure activities such as river rafting, mountain biking, and trekking. In the evening, relax by the bonfire at the hotel. Overnight stay at the hotel.

  • DAY 4: Nubra Valley to Leh

    After breakfast, check out from the hotel and drive to Nubra Valley via Khardung La Pass. Enjoy the scenic views and visit Diskit Monastery. Check-in at the camp for an overnight stay.

  • DAY 5: Departure

    After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to Leh Airport for your onward journey.

INCLUSIONS :

  • Accommodation for 4 nights in a deluxe hotel in Leh
  • Daily breakfast and dinner
  • All transfers and sightseeing by private vehicle
  • Visit to Shanti Stupa and Leh Palace
  • Adventure activities such as river rafting and trekking
  • Bonfire evening
  • Visit to Nubra Valley via Khardung La Pass
  • All applicable taxes

EXCLUSIONS :

  • Personal expenses such as laundry, tips, and phone calls
