Experience Kolakham: A Hidden Gem in the Eastern Himalayas :

Welcome to Kolakham, a picturesque village nestled in the tranquil embrace of the Neora Valley Reserve Forest, proudly standing as one of North Bengal's most captivating offbeat tourist destinations. Situated at an altitude of 6,500 feet above sea level, Kolakham offers a sanctuary for nature enthusiasts seeking to immerse themselves in the pristine beauty of the Eastern Himalayas.

Nature Retreat Amidst Serenity:

At Kolakham Nature Retreat, surrounded by lush Oak, Rhododendron, and Pine forests, you'll discover an ideal escape from the chaos of urban life. Each morning, awaken to the melodious symphony of birdsong, as the first rays of sunlight gently kiss the landscape. The retreat's cozy rooms, designed to capture the essence of the wilderness, provide sweeping views of verdant hills and the majestic snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas.

Embark on an Adventure:

For adventurous souls, Kolakham beckons with opportunities to explore the uncharted territories of the Neora Valley forest. Take on the challenge of jungle trails, trekking through pristine landscapes adorned with cascading waterfalls and breathtaking vistas. Marvel at the panoramic view of the awe-inspiring Kanchenjunga from the quaint village of Kolakham, and encounter the diverse wildlife that calls this region home.

A Haven for Nature Enthusiasts:

The Neora Valley forest, with its diverse flora and fauna, offers a sanctuary for wildlife enthusiasts and birdwatchers alike. Wander amidst a tapestry of orchids, rhododendrons, and bamboo groves, while keeping an eye out for elusive creatures such as Leopards, Himalayan Black Bears, and Ghoral. Delight in the sight of vibrant avian species, including Khaleej and Koklass Pheasants, and the majestic Golden Eagle soaring high above.

Location and Accessibility:

Located in the Darjeeling district, Kolakham is easily accessible from Lava, a mere 8 kilometers away. Situated at coordinates 27°06′05″N 88°40′40″E, this hidden gem invites travelers to embark on a journey of discovery amidst the serene landscapes of the Eastern Himalayas.

Embrace the Magic of Kolakham:

Whether you seek adventure, serenity, or simply a rendezvous with nature, Kolakham offers an unforgettable experience for galaxy travelers seeking to escape the ordinary and embrace the extraordinary beauty of the Eastern Himalayas. Come, immerse yourself in the splendor of Kolakham, where every moment is a testament to the timeless allure of the mountains.

ITINERARY :

  • Day 1: From NJP/IXB to Kolakham [107 KM - 3 HRS 30 MIN]

    Upon arrival at NJP, you'll be warmly welcomed and whisked away on a picturesque drive to Kolakham, a charming village cradled within the Neora Valley Reserve Forest, with the majestic Eastern Himalayan peaks as its backdrop. Nestled at an altitude of 6500ft, Kolakham is a haven for nature enthusiasts, enveloped by Oak, Rhododendron, and Pine forests. Your retreat at Kolakham Nature Retreat promises an immersion into serene mountain wilderness, where the symphony of bird songs greets you each morning. The highlight of the day is the breathtaking vista of Kanchenjunga, India's highest peak, visible from the quaint village of Kolakham. Embark on a mesmerizing jungle trail through the Neora forest, soaking in the pristine beauty of the surroundings. Don't miss the enchanting Change waterfalls, adding another layer to this unforgettable experience.

  • DAY 2 : KOLAKHAM FULL DAY SIGHTSEEING AND STAY

      Morning

    • Breakfast: Enjoy a hearty breakfast to fuel up for the day's adventures.

      • Sightseeing:

    • Changi Falls: Embark on a short 3 km jeep drive followed by a 1.5 km walk down through beautifully built staircases to reach Changi Falls. Immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of the falls nestled within the deep forest of Darjeeling Hills. Listen to the melodious chirping of rare tropical birds like Wagtail, Minivet, and others as you explore the area.

This journey is a paradise for nature lovers and bird watchers, with its dense tropical forests and an abundance of rare bird species. KHOLAKHAM CLIMATE:

Like any other hill station, Kolakham faces offseason during Monsoon. This place receives good rainfall, which makes the forest look green but results in landslides and road blockage with dense fog, leeches, and sticky mud. The best time to visit is from spring - summer or autumn - winter

  • Spring to Summer - March, April, May & June [5°C to 20°C]
  • Monsoon - July, August & September [12°C to 16°C]
  • Autumn - October & November [6°C to 14°C]
  • Winter - December, January & February [0°C to 8°C]
