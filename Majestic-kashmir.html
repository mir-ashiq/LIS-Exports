    OVERVIEW :

    Embark on a Spiritual Odyssey through Kashmir: Join us on a journey of inner peace and enlightenment with our Sacred Kashmir tour. Discover the mystical beauty of the Kashmir Valley, where snow-capped mountains, serene lakes, and lush valleys create the perfect backdrop for spiritual exploration. Visit ancient shrines and holy sites revered by devotees for centuries, including the revered Amarnath Cave and the historic Shankaracharya Temple. Experience the soothing tranquility of Dal Lake as you cruise along its pristine waters, and witness the vibrant culture of Sufism in the mystical city of Srinagar. Let us guide you on a transformative pilgrimage to Kashmir, where every moment is infused with divine energy and spiritual bliss.

    ITINERARY :

    • DAY 1 Kolkata – Jammu ( By train )

      Our tour manager will meet with you at Kolkata station, train departure time is 1145 hrs ( 11.45 AM). Same day we will provide you Lunch & dinner in train . Overnight at train

    • DAY 2 Train Journey
      • Our tour manager will provide you breakfast + Lunch + Dinner on time.
      • Overnight at train.
    • DAY 3 - Jammu arrival – Katra ( 50 Kms / 1.5 Hrs)

      Arrival at Jammu station, our driver picks up you and transfer to Katra . later check in at hotel & ready for the very early morning Journey for Vaishan Devi temple darshan.

    • DAY 4 Vaishno Devi Darshan ( 273 Kms / 7 Hrs)

      We will start the day with Vaishnomata Darshan. After returning from the temple we will take rest & overnight stay at hotel

    • DAY 5 Katra – Srinagar ( 273 Kms / 7 Hrs)

      After breakfast & refreshment our driver will picks up you and transfer to Srinagar. we will enjoy the scenic beauty of kashmir Valley enroute. Later check in at hotel & overnight stay at hotel.

    • DAY 6 Srinagar local sightseeing

      After breakfast from hotel then our driver picks up you and you explore Srinagar local tourist places. You visit Mughal Garden , Pari Mahal , Shankaracharya temple , Hazratbal Masjid , Chasma Shahi Garden, dal lake, Passing from Tulip garden. Then back to hotel & Overnight stay at hotel.

    • DAY 7 Srinagar – Sonmarg – Srinagar (81 Kms /2.5 Hrs)

      After breakfast from hotel then ready for your excursion tour to Sonmarg. Sonmarg is a hill station located in the Ganderbal District of Jammu and Kashmir, India. It is located about 62 kilometres from Ganderbal Town and 80 kilometres northeast of the capital city, Srinagar. Evening time back to hotel and Overnight stay at hotel

    • Day 8 – : Srinagar – Gulmarg – Srinagar

      After breakfast from hotel then ready for your excursion tour to Gulmarg. Gulmarg, known as Gulmarg in Kashmiri, is a town, hill station, popular tourist destination, popular skiing destination and a notified area committee in Baramulla district in the Indian union territory of Jammu and Kashmir. You can enjoy so many snow activities. Gondola Ride is the famous attraction at Gulmarg & The entire gondola ride covers about 5 kilometres. You can also book the gondola tickets online. Back to hotel and overnight stay at Houseboat.

    • Day 9 – : Srinagar - Pahalgam sightseeing (95 Kms/03 Hrs)

      After breakfast & check out from houseboat then drive to Pahalgam. Later check in at hotel & then explore Pahalgam. Pahalgam is famous for its scenic beauty and is the jewel of the picturesque Liddar valley in the high Himalayas. It provides an ideal setting for hiking, trekking, and fishing. It is also the starting point of the annual pilgrimage to the holy cave of Amarnath. You visit Betab Valley, Baisaran, Aru Valley. For Betab valley, Aru Valley local Srinagar car is not allowed in this route. if you will visit you can book directly at Pahalgam. Overnight stay at hotel

    • Day 10–: Pahalgam – Patnitop - Jammu station departure (245 Kms /6 Hrs) – Kolkata

      After breakfast & check out from hotel then drive to Jammu station for your final departure. Enroute, you visit Patnitop then drive to Jammu Station.

    • Day 11 – : Train Journey

      Our tour manager will provide you time to time food breakfast + lunch + dinner. Overnight at train

    • Day 12 – : Arrival Kolkata

      You will arrive at Kolkata station, our tour manager will provide you breakfast.
