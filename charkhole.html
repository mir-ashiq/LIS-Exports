Charkhole

Charkhole, nestled in the picturesque landscape of the Kalimpong subdivision, emerges as the latest gem in the offbeat tourist destination map of the Darjeeling district. Situated a mere 15 kilometers away from Lolegaon/Kafer and 26 kilometers from Kalimpong, this hidden paradise unveils its splendor with a panoramic 360-degree view of the majestic Mt. Kanchenjunga from the apex of Charkhole Hill.

The journey towards Charkhole is adorned with the embrace of a dense forest, where towering pine, oak, fir, and cypress trees stand tall, creating a mesmerizing canopy. Amongst this natural symphony, a variety of rhododendrons paint the landscape with their vibrant hues, while the melodious tunes of rare birds serenade travelers along the way.

At the heart of this natural wonderland lies the one and only Charkhole nature resort, a sanctuary of tranquility amidst the embrace of nature. Here, amidst the serene surroundings, travelers find solace and rejuvenation, as the beauty of the landscape unfolds before their eyes.

Charkhole isn't just a destination; it's a haven for those seeking respite from the chaos of urban life. Its secluded ambiance makes it the perfect hideaway, attracting honeymoon couples and nature enthusiasts alike. The allure of Charkhole lies in its untouched beauty, offering an escape from the mundane and a chance to reconnect with the pure essence of nature.

Moreover, Charkhole has also earned recognition beyond its natural charm. As the highest motor rally point in the entire region, it has become a favored destination for motor car rallies, adding a touch of adventure to its tranquil setting.

In recognition of its unparalleled beauty and potential as an offbeat destination, Charkhole has been honored with prestigious awards, including the title of "Most Promising Offbeat Destination." This accolade serves as a testament to the enchanting allure and untapped potential of this hidden paradise, inviting travelers to embark on a journey of discovery and exploration amidst the pristine wilderness of Charkhole.

Around Charkhole

Escape to Charkhole, a charming village nestled in the Himalayas, and experience the serenity of nature. With just three families residing here, tranquility is guaranteed.

Nature Enthusiasts Rejoice!

    1. Embark on nature trails through picturesque green forests teeming with diverse birdlife and rare flora.

    2. Explore the surrounding region with day trips to Lava, Lolegaon, Kolakham, and Rishop.

    3. Indulge in a gentle trek within Charkhole itself.

    4. Relax on the resort's expansive lawn with beautiful seating arrangements.

Unveiling the Wonders Nearby

Lolegaon/Kaffer: Witness breathtaking Himalayan panoramas. Witness the sunrise over snow-capped peaks from Jhandidara, and don't miss the region's only canopy walk!

Lava: Trek 4 km through the Neora Valley forest to reach Lava, the nearest town. Visit the majestic Lava Monastery and find unique handicrafts.

Neora Valley Forest:Embark on a thrilling jeep safari or trek deep into the forest. Nature lovers and birdwatchers will be enthralled by the diverse flora and fauna. For the adventurous, the Rachela Trek towards Aritar awaits.

Changi Falls:Witness the captivating Changi Falls nestled amidst the deep forest. A short jeep ride and walk down well-maintained stairs lead to this hidden gem.

Rishyap:Capture breathtaking views of snow-capped mountains like Kanchenjunga, Kabroo, Kharg, Pandim, Simvo, and Rathung.

Important Information

Climate: Pleasant summers (5-20°C) and cool winters (-5-10°C).

Altitude: 5,500 ft

Packing Essentials: Pack light with minimal clothing, toiletries, and necessities. Bring light woolens for summer and heavier woolens for winter.

Food and Drink

Carry bottled water as shops are scarce. Stock up on dry snacks like biscuits, chips, fruits, and nuts in Lava, Kalimpong, or Siliguri.

Alcohol is unavailable in Charkhole.

Staying Healthy

Pack essential medicines for common ailments like diarrhea and fever, along with bandages and antiseptic cream. The nearest medical facilities are in Lolegaon or Kalimpong.

Financial Matters

Cash is king! Credit cards, debit cards, and ATMs are not available in Charkhole.

Communication

Mobile network connectivity is limited. Consider carrying phones with different service providers for better reception.
