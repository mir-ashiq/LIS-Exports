OVERVIEW :

Discover the enchanting beauty of Gulmarg, a premier hill station and skiing destination in Jammu and Kashmir. Nestled in the Pir Panjal range, Gulmarg offers breathtaking views, lush meadows, and thrilling winter sports. Experience the charm of this picturesque town with our 4 nights and 5 days package, perfect for families and groups.

ITINERARY :

  • DAY 1: Arrival in Srinagar

    Arrive at Srinagar Airport and transfer to Gulmarg. Check-in at the hotel and enjoy a leisurely evening exploring the local market. Overnight stay at the hotel.

  • DAY 2: Gulmarg Sightseeing

    After breakfast, visit the famous Gulmarg Gondola, one of the highest cable cars in the world. Enjoy panoramic views and optional skiing or snowboarding. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

  • DAY 3: Adventure Activities

    Spend the day indulging in various adventure activities such as snowshoeing, snowmobiling, and ice skating. In the evening, relax by the bonfire at the hotel. Overnight stay at the hotel.

  • DAY 4: Gulmarg to Srinagar

    After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to Srinagar. Visit the Mughal Gardens, Shankaracharya Temple, and Dal Lake. Check-in at the houseboat for an overnight stay.

  • DAY 5: Departure

    After breakfast, check out from the houseboat and transfer to Srinagar Airport for your onward journey.

INCLUSIONS :

  • Accommodation for 4 nights in a deluxe hotel
  • Daily breakfast and dinner
  • All transfers and sightseeing by private vehicle
  • Gulmarg Gondola ride (Phase 1)
  • Bonfire evening
  • Houseboat stay in Srinagar
  • All applicable taxes

EXCLUSIONS :

  • Airfare/train fare
  • Personal expenses such as laundry, tips, and phone calls
  • Adventure activities not mentioned in the inclusions
  • Travel insurance
  • Any other expenses not mentioned in the inclusions
