Reshikhola

Reshikhola, also known as Rishi Khola or Rishikhola, offers a serene retreat amidst nature's bounty. Here's what makes it a unique destination:

Tranquil Environment: Reshikhola is nestled in a tranquil valley alongside the Reshi River, providing a serene atmosphere away from the hustle and bustle of city life. The calmness of the surroundings makes it an ideal destination for those seeking peace and relaxation.

Scenic Beauty: Surrounded by lush green forests and towering mountains, Reshikhola offers breathtaking views of pristine nature. The picturesque landscapes provide ample opportunities for photography and exploration.

Riverside Accommodation: The resort situated on the bank of the Reshi River offers a unique experience. Guests can enjoy the soothing sound of the flowing stream and the cool breeze from the river while relaxing in comfortable accommodations. It's an ideal choice for nature lovers seeking a close connection with the environment.

Nature Activities: Reshikhola offers various nature-related activities for tourists. Visitors can indulge in bathing in the crystal-clear waters of the Reshi River, which is not only refreshing but also therapeutic. Fishing in the river is another popular activity, allowing visitors to enjoy the tranquility of the surroundings while trying their hand at angling.

Silk Route Tour: Reshikhola lies within the Silk Route Tour, adding historical significance to its charm. Travelers can explore the ancient trade route and learn about its cultural and economic importance while experiencing the natural beauty of the region.

Overall, Reshikhola stands out as a destination where travelers can immerse themselves in the untouched beauty of nature, indulge in outdoor activities, and experience the tranquility of riverside living.

AROUND RESHIKHOLA

Tourist can go for a day-trip to Aritar, Lampokhri lake and Pedong.

Aritar:Aritar is an isolated place in East Sikkim is known for its scenic beauty and nearby Lampokhri Lake. It is around 1 hr drive from Reshi Khola.

Lampokhri Lake or Aritar lake:Lampokhri is one of the oldest natural lakes in Sikkim, located at an altitude of 4600ft. Tourists can enjoy boating here. It is the highest lake in Sikkim with a boating facility. It is also around 1 hr drive from Reshikhola.

Pedong:Pedong one of the oldest towns in historic Silk Route located at an altitude of 5000ft on the Kalimpong Algarah road. At Pedong you can explore Damsung fort, Pedong Monastery, Tinchule viewpoint, Riksum view point, etc. Pedong is around 1-hour drive from Reshikhola and it is the nearest town from ReshiKhola.
