Unveil the Enchanting Himalayas: A Rishyap Adventure

Rishyap, a hidden gem nestled in the Himalayas, offers a refreshing escape from the throngs of tourists. This offbeat destination in Darjeeling promises a haven of serenity amidst deep pine forests and a rich tapestry of flora and fauna.

Witness Breathtaking Sunrises and Panoramic Vistas

Embark on a captivating 3km trek through the dense woods. As you weave through bushes and boulders, the anticipation of the scenic payoff grows. Reach Tiffindara, the crown jewel of Rishyap, and be stunned by a magnificent sunrise painting the snow-capped peaks of Kanchenjunga, Kabru, and others in a mesmerizing golden hue.

A 360-Degree Spectacle for the Soul

Tiffindara isn't just about the sunrise. Take in a breathtaking 360-degree panorama. Spot towering peaks like Pandim, Simvo, and Narsingh. As dusk descends, witness a mesmerizing display of twinkling lights from nearby villages below.

Untouched Beauty Awaits

Rishyap's village tourism is relatively young, ensuring an unspoiled natural experience. Imagine waking up to mesmerizing views of Kanchenjunga from your cozy bed at LIS Exports' nature resort, perched atop a prime location.

Explore Nature's Wonders: Beyond Rishyap

Rishyap serves as a gateway to further adventures. For nature enthusiasts, a nature trail along the verdant forests bordering the Neora Valley National Park awaits. Encounter a variety of birds, unique flora, and even rare wildlife.

Craft Your Himalayan Escape

Feeling adventurous? Extend your exploration with onward journeys to Lava Lolegaon, Kolakham, or Charkhole, each offering distinct experiences. Rishyap beckons you to unwind, reconnect with nature, and create memories that will last a lifetime.

WHAT IS THE ALTITUDE OF RISHYAP?

The location of Rishyap is 8,500 Ft (2,591 meter) above Sea level. Coordinates is 27.1112° N, 88.6526° E

WHICH IS THE PERFECT TIME FOR RISHYAP TOUR BOOKING?

LIS Exports recommends specific seasons for traveling to Rishyap, considering weather conditions and tourist activities. Here's a breakdown of their suggestions:

Summer (April to July):

  • Ideal for summer holidays and vacation tours.
  • Pleasant weather with temperatures ranging from 18°C during the day to 8°C at night.
  • Clear skies offer a magnificent view of Mt. Kanchenjungha and other Himalayan peaks.
  • Blooms of rhododendron enhance the scenic beauty.
  • Early mornings are clear, perfect for witnessing spectacular sunrises from viewpoints like Tiffindara.

Monsoon (July to September):

  • Not recommended due to heavy rainfall, common landslides, and high chances of getting stranded.
  • Limited activities other than enjoying the rain indoors with hot beverages.

Winter (November to March):

  • Cold weather with temperatures ranging from 6°C during the day to 0°C at night.
  • Suitable for travelers who can withstand biting cold temperatures.

Autumn (October to November):

  • Considered the best season for Rishyap tours.
  • Guaranteed 100% crystal clear skies between the end of October and the first week of January.
  • Offers breathtaking views of Mt. Kanchenjungha at a handpicked distance.
  • Provides an everlasting experience for galaxy travelers with optimal weather conditions and stunning vistas.

LIS Exports assures an unforgettable experience during the autumn season, especially between October and November, with guaranteed clear skies and mesmerizing views of Mt. Kanchenjungha, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking a unique and memorable journey.

ITINERARY :

  • DAY 1 : NJP/IXB TO LAVA [3 HR 2 MIN (100.7 KM) VIA RISHI RD]

    Meet and greet at NJP / Bagdogra [IXB] and proceed for your Rishop Tour. First day visit Lava – a small hamlet nested about 2,200 m above sea level. Surrounded by Neora Valley National Park, this pristine place offers the serenity and tranquility with splendid Mt. Kanchenjungha view. En-route Lava experiences the stunning nature view. Hotel / Resort / Home stay check in. After lunch relax in the resort. In the evening visit Lava Monastery also known as Ratnashri Bihar Buddhist Gumpa established by Karma Lodra Rimpoche. Situated beside the Large Neora Reservoir Lake, this religious residence is created using brick and concrete. Due to the increase in the number of devotees and monks over the year, the facilities in Lava Monastery were expanded. Roam around the vicinity and get lost in the nature. Evening leisure and night stay at the Homestay in Lava.

  • DAY 2 : LAVA TO RISHYAP TRANSFER [19 MIN (5.2 KM) VIA LAVA RD/RISHI RD]

    After a belly full of breakfast, proceed towards Rishyap from your Lava Homestay. It is merely 30 min drive from Rishop among the thick pine forest. Innumerable and unknown birds will welcome you with whistle and chirping the entire journey. Literarily it is birders paradise. Rishyap is considered as one of the best tourist destinations in Himalaya. Apart from thick pine forest, Rishop offers splendid view of Mt. Kanchenjunga and other snow capped mountain peaks. Enjoy the surrounding, visit local village and experience their life style, culture and livelihood. Evening free for leisure. You can step up in local dance and folk song with local villagers. Night stay at the Resort / Home stay in Rishyap.

  • DAY 3 : RISHYAP FULL DAY TOUR & LEISURE

    Wake up early in the morning. After freshen up let’s have a morning stroll towards Tiffindara – a wonderful view point just 700 ft above from Rishyap. Tiffindara offers spectacular view of Mt. Kanchenjungha along with others peak around it like Mt.Pandim, Mt Simbhu, etc. From every nook and corner of the viewpoint and from the spaces of pine trees you can find out at least one mountain peak. It is wonderful experience one can enjoy and will be a lifelong gift.

    Enjoy the tranquility and serenity of the place, listen to the bird, let's find out how many of them you can recognize. Roam around the serpentine path drenched with mystic fog. Smell it, get lost in it. Discover you self in a completely new way. See the smile of local children, intake pure oxygen as much as you can.

    Evening at leisure. Enjoy bonfire and some local delicacy with hot tea or Coffee. Sing loud no matter how bad you are. Dance – don’t care, no body to judge you. Live life large. Dinner and sweet dream.

  • DAY 4 : RISHYAP TO KOLAKHAM [47 MIN (13.7 KM)]

    Rise up early in the morning to get spectacle sunrise view and glittering Kanchenjungha. Breathe fresh air. After morning stroll, have your breakfast and proceed towards Kolakham a god gifted village of Mother Nature nestled in the dense forest of Naora Valley National Park. It is merely one hrs drive from Rishyap. Enroute enjoy the scenic beauty through dense pine forest. After hotel check-in, relax. Post lunch visit major attractions in Kolakham -

    Changi Falls - Just 3 km drive and 1.5 km walk down through nicely built staircases and rest hut takes you to the most beautiful falls in the deep forest of Naora Valley National Park. It is the only falls in the deep forest which can be reached by jeep and a walk down to around 250 stairs. In the deep forest of Neora Valley with sounds of rare tropical birds like Wagtail, Minivet and others make your journey a memorable one.Neora Valley Forest - A jungle safari by Jeep and trek inside the forest is a wonderful experience. This trip is a must to all nature lovers and bird watchers. The trips through the deep jungle, full of tropical forest and most rare birds will make this trip memorable one.

  • DAY 5 : TRANSFER TO NJP / BAGDOGRA / SILIGURI

    Morning is free personal enjoyment and enjoys the flora and fauna of some rare species in Kolakham. After Lunch start for NJP / Bagdogra / Siliguri with a sweet vacation memory.
