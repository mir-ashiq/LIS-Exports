OVERVIEW :

Embark on a Sacred Journey to Ayodhya: Explore the spiritual heart of India with our Ayodhya tour. Delve into the rich tapestry of Hindu mythology and history as you visit revered temples, ancient sites, and sacred shrines. Witness the serene beauty of the Sarayu River and immerse yourself in the divine atmosphere of this legendary city. Pay homage at the birthplace of Lord Rama, a site steeped in myth and legend, and experience the timeless devotion of pilgrims from around the world. Let us guide you on a transformative pilgrimage to Ayodhya, where every step is a prayer and every moment a connection to the divine.

ITINERARY :

  • DAY 1 ARRIVE LUCKNOW

    Arrive at Lucknow Airport, On arrival driver will pick you and transfer to hotel. After fresh up proceed for Lucknow sightseeing includes Chota Imambara, bada Imambara, The Residency, Clock Tower, Ambedkar Park, etc. Evening is free for own activities. Overnight stay in hotel

  • DAY 2 LUCKNOW- AYODHYA

    After breakfast check out from hotel and drive to Ayodhya. It will take around 3 hrs to reach Ayodhya. On arrival check in hotel. After fresh up visit Ramjanmabhumi - the birthplace of lord Rama, Janki bhawan, Kanak bhawan, Hanuman Garhi, Sarayu River. Overnight in hotel.

  • DAY 3 - AYODHYA - PRAYAGRAJ

    After breakfast check out from hotel and proceed to visit Later drive to Prayagraj. It will take around 5 hrs to reach Prayagraj. On arrival check in hotel. After fresh up proceed to visit Anand bhawan, Chandrashekhar Azad Park. Overnight stay and dinner in hotel.

  • DAY 4 PRAYAGRAJ - CHITRAKOOT - PRAYAGRAJ

    After breakfast proceed for the excursion of Chitrakoot, it will take about 3 hrs to reach Chitrakoot, On arrival visit Ramghat, Kamadgiri, Ram Bharat Milap, Janaki Kund, Hanuman Dhara etc. Evening drive back to Prayagraj. Overnight stay and dinner in Prayagraj.

  • DAY 5 PRAYAGRAJ - VARANASI

    Early Morning proceed for Triveni sangam, followed by Hanuman temple & Allahabad fort, later back to hotel and have breakfast. After breakfast, check out from hotel and drive to Varanasi. On arrival check in hotel. In the evening, get ready to experience the Evening Ganga Aarti Ceremony at Dashashwamedh Ghat. Get a glimpse of the spiritualism at the Ganga Ghats (river front). Enjoy the Ganga Aarti, late evening back to hotel. Overnight stay in hotel.

  • DAY 6 VARANASI- SIGHTSEEING

    Early in the morning proceed to visit the most religious Kashi Vishwanath Temple, Annapurna Temple, Kaal Bhairav. Later back to hotel for breakfast. After breakfast proceed to visit Vishalakshi devi, Bharat Mata Temple, Durga Kund, Sankat Mochan, (Hanuman Temple). Eveningv is free for own activities, overnight stay in hotel. Night stay at Hotel in Varanasi

  • DAY 7 VARANASI DEPART

    After breakfast check out from the hotel and free for own activities, later transfer to Varanasi Airport/ Railway Station. Tour End
