OVERVIEW :

Experience the epitome of modern luxury and ancient charm on a Dubai tour. Marvel at the iconic skyline dominated by the world's tallest building, the Burj Khalifa, and indulge in opulent shopping at the Dubai Mall. Discover the cultural treasures of the old city in the historic Al Fahidi neighborhood and the bustling souks of Deira. Dive into adventure with desert safaris, dune bashing, and camel rides, or unwind on pristine beaches along the Arabian Gulf. With its blend of futuristic architecture, rich heritage, and endless entertainment options, Dubai offers an unforgettable journey where every moment is infused with grandeur and excitement.

ITINERARY :

  • DAY 1

    Welcome to the United Arab Emirates! Upon arrival at Dubai Airport, you'll be warmly greeted and escorted to your hotel. Check-in and settle in for your stay. Enjoy a delicious lunch at an Indian restaurant. Later in the evening, embark on a serene Dhow Cruise along the historic Dubai Creek.

  • DAY 2

    Start your day with a half-day Dubai City Tour, exploring the vibrant city's highlights. Visit the iconic Dubai Frame and marvel at its architectural beauty. Enjoy lunch at an Indian restaurant before ascending to the 124th floor of the Burj Khalifa during non-prime hours for breathtaking views. Conclude the day with a sumptuous dinner at an Indian restaurant.

  • DAY 3

    Indulge in a thrilling Desert Safari adventure, including exhilarating dune bashing and a delicious BBQ dinner under the starlit desert sky. Lunch will be served at an Indian restaurant, ensuring a delightful culinary experience. Visit Future Museaum(Tickets at your own cost)

  • DAY 4

    Explore the whimsical Miracle Garden, a floral paradise showcasing intricate designs and vibrant colors. Then, immerse yourself in the cultural extravaganza of Global Village, experiencing diverse traditions from around the world. Refuel with lunch and dinner at Indian restaurants, savoring authentic flavors.

  • DAY 5

    Embark on an enriching Abu Dhabi City Tour, where you'll visit the majestic Sheikh Zayed Mosque, a masterpiece of Islamic architecture. Enjoy lunch and dinner at Indian restaurants, catering to your culinary preferences.

  • DAY 6

    Check out from your hotel after a memorable stay in Dubai. Relish a final lunch at an Indian restaurant before bidding farewell to this dynamic city. Your journey concludes with a drop-off at Dubai Airport, filled with cherished memories and experiences
