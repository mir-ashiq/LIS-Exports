    AROUND SILLERY GAON

    Tourist can go for either soft trek to the following places or a day-trip to Aritar, Lampokhri Lake and Pedong.

    Ramitey View Point:A soft trek to Ramitey, the longest view point of river Teesta, close to 14 turns of Testa at a glance. This is around 30 mins soft trek from Sillery Gaon. Most of the road is also motorable and beauty of this road towards Ramitey is through deep forest and along with the Mt. Kanchenjunga.

    Tinchuley Top: Tourists can also go for another soft trek to Tinchuley View Point. Tinchuley is famous for a perfect 360 degree angle view of the surrounding majestic mountains, including Mt. Kanchenjunga.

    Damsang Fort: It is the fort of last Lepcha King and the only fort in the hills of Darjeeling and Sikkim. A Soft Trek of around 45 mins to 1 hr will lead to Damsang Fort.

    Aritar: Aritar an isolated place in East Sikkim is known for its scenic beauty and nearby Lampokhri Lake. It is around 1.5 hr drive from Sillery.

    Lampokhri Lake: Lampokhri is one of the oldest natural lakes in Sikkim, located at an altitude of 4600ft.Tourist can enjoy boating here. It is highest lake in Sikkim with boating facility. It is also around 1.5 hr drive from SilleryGaon.

    Pedong: Pedong one of the oldest towns in historic Silk Route located at an altitude of 5000ft on the Kalimpong Algarah road. At Pedong you can explore Damsung fort, Pedong Monastery, Tinchule view point, Riksum view point etc. Pedong is around 30 mins drive from Sillery Gaon and it is nearest town from SilleryGaon.
