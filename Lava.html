Lava

Lava

Embark on a Cosmic Journey! Book Your Lava package with LIS Exports Now 🌌

Craving a getaway where tranquility meets untamed wilderness? Look no further than Lava, nestled near Kalimpong in the Darjeeling district of West Bengal. With its lush green valleys, mist-shrouded landscapes, and a symphony of bird songs, Lava promises an escape like no other.

Perched at an altitude of over 7000 feet, Lava boasts not of flashy attractions but of a serene ambiance that beckons nature enthusiasts. As the gateway to the Neora Valley National Park, it flaunts a rich tapestry of flora and fauna, offering endless opportunities for exploration.

The journey to Lava itself is a visual feast, with every mile revealing a gradual transition from tropical deciduous to verdant alpine forests of pine, fir, and birch. Keep your eyes peeled, for the Himalayan avian wonders might grace your sight along the way.

Arriving at Lava feels like stepping into a verdant oasis, embraced by towering emerald hills and veiled in a mystical cloak of mist and clouds. Leave the hustle and bustle behind as you embark on romantic strolls along hilly trails, hand in hand with your beloved.

The charm of Lava lies not in its grandeur but in its simplicity, enticing you to shed the shackles of everyday life and surrender to the allure of the wild. Lose yourself amidst the whispering pines or embark on a leisurely walk through the enchanting pine forests, letting nature weave its spell around you.

Unveiling the Enchantment of Lava: A Traveler's Guide

Unveil Spiritual Serenity at Lava Monastery: Perched atop a hill, Lava Monastery beckons pilgrims and sightseers alike. Its majestic presence dominates the landscape, offering a serene start to your day.

Embrace Nature's Symphony at Rangbang Valley & Rachet Reserve Forest: Calling all birding enthusiasts! Grab your binoculars and cameras to capture the vibrant symphony of birds in Rangbang Valley and Rachet Reserve Forest. From Hill Partridges to soaring Black Eagles, prepare to be dazzled by the diverse avian population.

Witness Nature's Grand Finale at Ghanti Dara & Tiffin Dara: As the sun dips below the horizon, paint your day with a romantic touch. Ghanti Dara and Tiffin Dara, two enchanting hilltops, offer breathtaking sunset vistas, leaving you with memories that linger.

Lava: Your Gateway to Enchanting Escapes: Lava serves as your perfect base camp for exploring the region's hidden gems. Lolegaon, Rishyap, Kolakham, Charkhole, and Jhandi Dara await, each boasting its own unique charm.

Climate: Brace yourself for cool climes. Summers hover between a crisp 5°C and a pleasant 20°C, while winters can dip to a chilly -2°C, reaching a maximum of 8°C.

Altitude: Get ready to breathe in fresh mountain air, as Lava sits at an impressive 7,000 ft above sea level.

Packing Essentials: Pack light! Opt for minimal clothing, toiletries, and essentials. Pack light woolens for summer and heavier options for winter.

Food & Drinks: Stay hydrated with bottled water brought from Kalimpong or Siliguri. Consider carrying dry snacks like biscuits, chips, and dried fruits. Foreign liquor is available in Rishyap.

Going Cashless? Think Again: Leave your credit cards and ATM dreams behind. Cash is king in Lava!

Staying Connected: Though major mobile operators cover Lava, reception can be patchy. Consider carrying phones with different service providers for better connectivity.
