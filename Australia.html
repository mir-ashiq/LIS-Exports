OVERVIEW :

Embark on an unforgettable adventure Down Under with an Australia tour. Explore the vibrant cityscapes of Sydney and Melbourne, where iconic landmarks like the Sydney Opera House and the Great Ocean Road await. Immerse yourself in the ancient culture of the Aboriginal people and witness the breathtaking beauty of the Outback's red desert landscapes. Dive into the colorful underwater world of the Great Barrier Reef, or encounter unique wildlife such as kangaroos and koalas in their natural habitats. Whether you're seeking urban sophistication, rugged wilderness, or laid-back beach vibes, Australia offers a kaleidoscope of experiences that will leave you enchanted and inspired.

ITINERARY :

  • Day 1 Arrive In Melbourne

    Welcome to Australia! After arriving at Melbourne Tullamarine Airport transfer to Hotel and complete check-in procedure. Tonight, enjoy dinner at a local Indian restaurant in Melbourne. Overnight in Melbourne.

  • Day 2: Orientation Tour Of Melbourne - Afternoon Philip Island Tour (General View) | (Closed On Sunday)

    After breakfast at the hotel, we proceed to discover the breath-taking landmarks and monuments of Melbourne on your city tour, which includes an orientation tour of the Royal Botanical Gardens, Town Hall, St. Patrick’s Cathedral, National Tennis centre, the 1956 Olympic venue, the Shrine of Remembrance Bourke Street Mall and some beautifully restored Victorian houses. Explore the inner sanctum of the legendary MCG on a behind-the-scenes tour. Venture into the player changing rooms and step onto the arena itself. Meet the guide at the Melbourne Cricket Grounds for this tour that takes you behind the scenes for 1 hour and 15 minutes. Visit parts of the stadium that the general public doesn't get to see with a guide who is an MCC member.

    Today after lunch get ready to encounter the cutest aquatic animals alive at the Phillip Island Penguin Parade. It is one of Australia’s most popular wildlife attractions - perfect for a family holiday or getting back to nature. Every sunset, wild little penguins emerge from the sea and waddle across the beach to their sand dune burrows

    Tonight enjoy dinner at a local Indian restaurant in Melbourne.

    Overnight in Melbourne

  • DAY 3 Melbourne Great Ocean Road Tour

    Today after breakfast, head on for one of the most spectacular coastal drives – Great Ocean Road tour. See the abundance of Australian wildlife, whilst checking out the magnificent surf beaches, and walk amongst the ancient rainforest, before arriving in the afternoon to witness the marvel of these gigantic limestone stacks known as the 12 Apostles located in Port Campbell National Park. Tonight enjoy dinner at a local Indian restaurant in Melbourne. Overnight in Melbourne

  • Day 4: Flight To Cairns. Onto Cairns – The Tropical Paradise. Today, Get Set For A Day Of Fun And Excitement As You Visit Kuranda - A Village In The Rainforest.

    After an American breakfast at the hotel or a boxed breakfast, we transfer you to the airport for your flight to Cairns. On arrival enjoy lunch. Later proceed to Kuranda – a World Heritage Rainforest. Experience one way Skyrail journey that immerses you in an intimate experience where you will relax and enjoy the stunning rainforest scenery, panoramic views of the Cairns tropical region and the glittering waters of the Coral Sea. After a day of excitement and fun, we return to Cairns. Enjoy a dinner at a local Indian restaurant. Overnight in Cairns.

  • Day 5: Join Us On An Unforgettable Experience To The Great Barrier Reef – The Only Living Structure Visible From The Space

    This morning after an early breakfast at your hotel, we proceed to board the cruise to the Green Island. Escape into a world of surprises to experience the breathtaking beauty of the Great Barrier Reef. There is an abundance of activities for swimmers and non-swimmers to enjoy. A chance to Snorkel and dive, or you can stay dry and explore the stunning reef from the comfort of an air-conditioned semi- submersible that will take you to an underwater world that has a dazzling kaleidoscope of colours. Enjoy continental lunch on board the cruise. Return to your hotel with fantastic memories of this living natural wonder. Later in the evening visit Cairns night market on your own. Tonight, a delicious dinner awaits you at a local Indian restaurant. Overnight in Cairns.

  • Day 6: Transfer From Cairns To Gold Coast - Jet Boat Ride. Admission To Skypoint Observation Deck.

    After breakfast we proceed to for airport to board your flight to Gold Coast. Next have some fun and excitement as you enjoy 360° spin, on a high speed drifting Jet Boat ride on backwaters of Gold Coast. It is a great way to see what the glitzy Gold Coast is all about. Later proceed to Skypoint observation Deck. SkyPoint is an observation deck located 230 metres off the ground at the top of the Q1 on the Gold Coast. Skypoint Observation Deck is Australia’s only beachside observation deck. Take in expansive views of the Gold Coast coastline, the Gold Coast Hinterlands, and the Pacific Ocean from Australia’s tallest building. Overnight in Gold coast

  • Day 7:Enjoy Movie World - Fly To Sydney

    After an American breakfast at the hotel, check-out & proceed to MOVIE WORLD , MOVIE WORLD the largest movie based theme park in the Gold Coast region. The theme park features several themed zones along with many DC Superheroes, villains and much more. This theme park will give you an opportunity to meet your favorite stars and click many pictures with them. Visit the Batman's Legacy where many props, cars and costumes are on display here. Later Early Evening - Brisbane airport to take your flight to Sydney. Enjoy dinner at an Indian Restaurant. Overnight in Sydney.

  • Day 8: City Tour Of Sydney - Enjoy A Spectacular Guided Tour Of The World renowned Sydney Opera House. And Discover Stunning Underwater Life At The Sydney Aquarium And Visit Sydney Wildlife Park. Visit The World Famous Madame Tussauds Wax Museum And Sydney Tower

    After breakfast take a journey of an exciting city tour of Sydney which includes highlights like the Darling Harbour, one of the world’s greatest waterfront destinations; experience a slice of the city’s history at the Rocks area, the busy CBD area, Hyde Park, the Harbour Bridge, Mrs. Macquaries Chair and the world renowned Bondi beach

    through the world famous Sydney Aquarium and the Wildlife Park. Also experience the world of glamour, glitz, sport, politics and music when you visit the world famous Madame Tussauds Wax Museum where you will see the life size wax statues of the famous personalities from all around the world. Next visit Sydney Tower before checking into your hotel. We also take you on a guided tour of one the world’s most spectacular landmark – the Sydney Opera House. Enjoy dinner at an Indian Restaurant.

    Overnight in Sydney

  • Day 9 Free In The Most Amazing City Of Australia! Have The Time Of Your Life As You Get Set For The Most Exciting, Entertaining And Fun Night On Showboat Dinner Cruise Along Sydney Harbour!

    OPTIONAL - Today you have your day at leisure at the most happening city of Australia. You can enjoy the many adventures in Sydney on your own OR Sydney Bridge climb.

    After an American breakfast at the hotel, you have the opportunity to spend time at your own pace. You can shop at the classic Queen Victoria building – the most beautiful shopping centre in southern hemisphere on your own OR you can experience the spectacular blue-hazed beauty, dramatic cliffs and deep canyons of the Blue Mountains OR see Sydney from above the most iconic bridge in the world – the Sydney Bridge. The fully guided climb takes you to the summit of this world famous bridge, 134 meters above Sydney Harbour. Tonight, get set for a lifetime experience on board the spectacular Showboat dinner cruise. Be captivated by the breath-taking scenery as you sail through the shimmering Sydney Harbour with its mesmerizing panorama of bright city lights, Opera House and the famous Harbour Bridge.

    OPTIONAL - Blue Mountain, On this captivating day tour of the Blue Mountains, a World Heritage, admire the breathtaking beauty & discover Australia's nature’s magnificence. Go to Featherdale Fauna Park for close experiences with Australia's lovely wildlife and then to Scenic World for stunning views of the Blue Mountains. Blue Mountains Day Trip will be a treat to eyes for nature lovers. So, Book the Blue Mountains Tour to see the region's stunning natural splendour.

    Experience fine dining with Indian / International menu on board followed by a dazzling theatre cabaret featuring professional Australian showgirls in their glamorous costumes! Enjoy dinner at an Indian Restaurant

    Overnight in Sydney

  • Day 10: Fly Back With Pleasant Memories

    After buffet/boxed breakfast, we check out and proceed to the airport for your flight back home.
