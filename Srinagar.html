OVERVIEW :

Discover the enchanting beauty of Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir. Nestled in the Kashmir Valley, Srinagar is known for its beautiful gardens, lakes, and houseboats. Experience the charm of this picturesque city with our 4 nights and 5 days package, perfect for families and groups.

ITINERARY :

  • DAY 1: Arrival in Srinagar

    Arrive at Srinagar Airport and transfer to your hotel in Srinagar. Check-in at the hotel and enjoy a leisurely evening exploring the local market. Overnight stay at the hotel.

  • DAY 2: Srinagar Sightseeing

    After breakfast, visit the famous Mughal Gardens, Shankaracharya Temple, and Dal Lake. Enjoy a Shikara ride on Dal Lake. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

  • DAY 3: Adventure Activities

    Spend the day indulging in various adventure activities such as trekking, paragliding, and river rafting. In the evening, relax by the bonfire at the hotel. Overnight stay at the hotel.

  • DAY 4: Gulmarg Exploration

    After breakfast, enjoy a day trip to Gulmarg. Explore the beautiful meadows and take a ride on the Gulmarg Gondola. Return to Srinagar in the evening and check-in at the houseboat for an overnight stay.

  • DAY 5: Departure

    After breakfast, check out from the houseboat and transfer to Srinagar Airport for your onward journey.

INCLUSIONS :

  • Accommodation for 4 nights in a deluxe hotel in Srinagar
  • Daily breakfast and dinner
  • All transfers and sightseeing by private vehicle
  • Shikara ride on Dal Lake
  • Bonfire evening
  • Houseboat stay in Srinagar
  • All applicable taxes

EXCLUSIONS :

  • Airfare/train fare
  • Personal expenses such as laundry, tips, and phone calls
  • Adventure activities not mentioned in the inclusions
  • Travel insurance
  • Any other expenses not mentioned in the inclusions
