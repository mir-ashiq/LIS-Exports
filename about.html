About Us

Welcome to LIS Exports

Embark on a journey beyond the ordinary with LIS Exports, a super-specialty Himalayan Destination Management Company nestled in the heart of Kolkata, India. Our mission is to curate unforgettable experiences that transcend the realms of imagination, catering to the discerning needs of every traveler.

About Us:

At LIS Exports, we pride ourselves on our unrivaled expertise in the Himalayan region, coupled with an unwavering commitment to excellence. As the leading tour operator for Bhutan in India, we have earned the trust and respect of our clients by offering bespoke Bhutan Package Tours that unveil the hidden treasures of this enchanting kingdom.

Our Services:

  • Bhutan Package Tours: Explore the mystique of Bhutan with our meticulously crafted package tours, including Bhutan Group Tours and custom itineraries tailored to your preferences.
  • Himalayan Escapes: Journey through the rugged landscapes of Arunachal Pradesh, Ladakh, Sikkim, and more with our expertly curated adventure tours.
  • Exotic International Getaways: Indulge in the allure of distant lands with our international tour packages, featuring Bali, Maldives, Bangkok Pattaya, Dubai, Egypt, Sri Lanka, and beyond.
  • Weekend Adventures: Experience adrenaline-pumping escapades with our thrilling weekend tours to Sundarbans, Sandakphu, Silk Route, and beyond.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expertise: Our team boasts unparalleled knowledge of the terrain, ensuring seamless travel experiences.
  • Personalization: We understand that every traveler is unique, and thus, we tailor our services to exceed your expectations.
  • Trustworthiness: Registered with the Tourism Council of Bhutan, we uphold the highest standards of integrity and professionalism.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our utmost priority is to ensure that you cherish your holidays forever, leaving you with memories to last a lifetime.
