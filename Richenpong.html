    Richenpong

    Rinchenpong Tour offers a serene escape into the heart of nature, away from the hustle and bustle of city life. Imagine waking up to the soothing melody of birdsong, with the majestic peaks of Kanchenjunga greeting you from your window. In Rinchenpong, it's just you, your beloved, and the breathtaking beauty of the Himalayas.

    Nestled at an altitude of over 5576 feet, Rinchenpong promises tranquility at its best. Here, you can take leisurely strolls through forested areas and hill slopes, with the snow-capped peaks standing tall as your companions. The town may not boast of bustling markets or crowded streets, but it offers something far more precious – a chance to reconnect with nature and with each other.

    As you explore Rinchenpong, you'll come across hidden gems like the Bike-Pokhri, Rinchenpong Monastery, Ati Buddha, and Rabindra Smriti Van, each offering moments of charm and serenity. Whether you're gazing at the panoramic views of the valleys below or admiring the snow-clad peaks on the horizon, every moment spent here is a memory to cherish.

    Rinchenpong is not just a destination; it's an experience tailor-made for couples seeking a romantic getaway. Whether you're celebrating your honeymoon or simply escaping for a few days of bliss, Rinchenpong welcomes you with open arms. So why not make your journey to Sikkim truly unforgettable by discovering the magic of Rinchenpong?

    ITINERARY :

    • DAY 1 : ARRIVE BAGDOGRA / NJP AND TRANSFER TO PELLING [125 KMS/ 5 HRS]

      Embark on an unforgettable journey as you arrive at Bagdogra Airport [IXB] / NJP Railway Station, where our warm hospitality awaits you. From there, we whisk you away to your first destination: Pelling. Nestled just 125 km away from NJP or Bagdogra, in the picturesque western part of Sikkim, Pelling greets you with its tranquil charm and breathtaking views.

      Perched at an altitude of over 7000 feet, Pelling offers a panoramic vista of the majestic Mt. Kanchenjunga, where the day unfolds with the mesmerizing spectacle of the snowy peaks transitioning from pink to gold to white, and concludes with a magnificent display of fiery orange hues.

      Upon arrival, settle into your comfortable accommodations at the hotel, where you can unwind and soak in the serene ambiance. The evening beckons with the promise of leisurely exploration; take a leisurely stroll through the enchanting hamlet, immersing yourself in its beauty and tranquility.

      Experience a night of relaxation and rejuvenation as you retire to your cozy abode, enveloped by the serene ambiance of Pelling. Tomorrow holds new adventures, but tonight, let the tranquility of Pelling embrace you."

    • DAY 2 : FULL DAY PELLING SIGHTSEEING TOUR

      Rabdentse Ruins: Immerse yourself in history as you visit the ruins of Rabdentse, the second capital of the former Kingdom of Sikkim. Surrounded by dense forests, these ancient ruins offer a glimpse into the region's royal past, with remnants of palaces, temples, and monasteries dotting the landscape.

      Sewaro Rock Garden: Experience the tranquility of nature at Sewaro Rock Garden, a serene spot adorned with lush greenery, cascading waterfalls, and unique rock formations. Take a leisurely stroll along the pathways, breathe in the fresh mountain air, and marvel at the natural beauty that surrounds you.

      Pemayangtse Monastery Delve into the spiritual heart of Sikkim with a visit to Pemayangtse Monastery, one of the oldest and most prestigious monasteries in the state. Admire the exquisite architecture, intricate woodwork, and ancient relics housed within its sacred halls, and take a moment to soak in the peaceful atmosphere of this sacred site.

      Singshore Bridge:Prepare for an adrenaline rush as you venture to Singshore Bridge, one of the highest suspension bridges in Asia. Spanning across a deep gorge amidst lush greenery, this engineering marvel offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and valleys, making it a must-visit for thrill-seekers and nature enthusiasts alike.

      Changey Waterfall: Conclude your day of exploration with a visit to Changey Waterfall, a hidden gem tucked away in the verdant forests of Pelling. Marvel at the sight of crystal-clear waters cascading down rugged cliffs, surrounded by pristine wilderness and serenity. Take a moment to immerse yourself in the natural beauty of this enchanting waterfall before returning to your hotel for a well-deserved rest.

      Experience the Tranquility of Sangacholing Monastery:As night falls over Pelling, embark on a serene journey to Sangacholing Monastery, perched atop a hilltop amidst lush greenery. The tranquil ambiance of this sacred site creates the perfect setting for meditation and reflection. Gaze upon the illuminated Buddha statues as they glow in the darkness, offering a sense of peace and spiritual awakening.

      Marvel at the Pelling Skywalk: Step into the realm of engineering marvels with a visit to the Pelling Skywalk, a relatively new attraction that promises breathtaking views of Mt. Kanchenjunga and the surrounding landscape. Traverse the suspended walkway high above the valley floor, feeling as though you're walking among the clouds. Let the awe-inspiring vistas of the majestic Himalayas leave you spellbound and inspired.

      Sunset Picnic at Rabdentse Ruins:As the golden hues of twilight paint the sky, embark on a scenic journey to Rabdentse Ruins for a memorable sunset picnic. Situated amidst dense forests and commanding panoramic views of Khangchendzonga and the valley below, this ancient capital of the Sikkim Royalty provides the perfect backdrop for an evening of relaxation and reflection. Savor delicious local delicacies as you bask in the beauty of nature and immerse yourself in the rich history of this fascinating site.

      Stargazing and Bonfire:End your night on a cozy note with a stargazing session around a crackling bonfire at your hotel in Pelling. Away from the city lights, the night sky above Pelling comes alive with twinkling stars and constellations, offering a mesmerizing spectacle for astronomy enthusiasts and romantics alike. Share stories, laughter, and warmth by the fire as you create unforgettable memories under the vast expanse of the Himalayan night sky.

    • DAY 3 - PELLING TO RINCHENPONG TRANSFER [1 HR 47 MIN (45.6 KM) VIA PELLING - DENTAM RD]

      As you bid farewell to the enchanting vistas of Pelling, embark on a journey to Rinchenpong, the Silent Valley of Sikkim, where time seems to stand still amidst nature's grandeur.

      Upon arrival, let the tranquility of Rinchenpong envelop you as you settle into your chosen accommodation, whether it's a cozy resort or a charming homestay. Surrounded by dense forests and framed by the majestic silhouette of Mt. Kanchenjunga, Rinchenpong beckons you to unwind and immerse yourself in its serene ambiance.

      After indulging in a delectable midday meal prepared with fresh local ingredients, set out to explore the hidden treasures of Rinchenpong. Take a leisurely stroll along the forestry paths adorned with fluttering prayer flags, each whispering wishes of peace and prosperity into the gentle breeze. Allow yourself to be captivated by the symphony of nature as you traverse through this untouched paradise.

      As the afternoon unfolds, find yourself enchanted by the mesmerizing beauty of Rinchenpong. From the vibrant hues of the flora to the majestic presence of the towering mountains, every corner of this silent valley offers a glimpse into the raw splendor of Mother Nature. Take a moment to pause, breathe, and appreciate the simple joys of life amidst such breathtaking surroundings.

      As the sun dips below the horizon, painting the sky with hues of orange and pink, retreat to your accommodation for a restful overnight stay. Let the soothing sounds of the forest lull you into a peaceful slumber, as you dream of the adventures that await you in the silent valley of Rinchenpong.

    • DAY 4 : RINCHENPONG TO RAVANGLA TRANSFER [1 HR 59 MIN (59.0 KM)]

      After a hearty breakfast, set out on a picturesque journey to Ravangla, a charming town nestled in the southern part of Sikkim. Situated at an altitude of over 7000 feet, Ravangla welcomes you with open arms, offering a perfect blend of simplicity, serenity, and awe-inspiring views of the snow-capped peaks that adorn the northern horizon.

      As you arrive in Ravangla, let the tranquility of this idyllic town wash over you, soothing your senses and rejuvenating your spirit. Check into your accommodation and take a moment to soak in the breathtaking surroundings that stretch as far as the eye can see. Whether you choose to stay in a cozy guesthouse or a quaint homestay, rest assured that your night in Ravangla promises comfort and relaxation.

      With the afternoon free to explore at your leisure, immerse yourself in the natural beauty and tranquility that Ravangla has to offer. Take a leisurely stroll through the town's winding pathways, adorned with vibrant prayer flags fluttering in the gentle breeze. Pause to admire the lush greenery that blankets the landscape, punctuated by the majestic peaks that rise majestically against the azure sky.

      Indulge in moments of quiet reflection as you find solace in the serene ambiance of Ravangla. Whether you choose to meditate amidst the tranquil surroundings, capture the beauty of nature through your lens, or simply unwind with a good book, let the peaceful atmosphere of this charming town envelop you in its embrace.

      As the day transitions into night, revel in the tranquility of Ravangla under the starry sky. With the crisp mountain air filling your lungs and the soothing sounds of nature serenading you to sleep, drift off into a restful slumber, knowing that you have experienced the true essence of this hidden gem in the heart of Sikkim.

    • DAY 5 : RAVANGLA TO GANGTOK TRANSFER [2 HR 44 MIN (67.5 KM)]

      Post breakfast start for Gangtok Tour. On the way visit - Buddha Park, Bon Monastery, Ralang Monastery, Ralong Hot Springs, Temi Tea Estate. On arrival Gangtok, check in to the beautiful view rooms of the hotel well equipped with modern amenities and sink into the soft bed to get off the exhaustion. In the evening, take a stroll in the neighborhood or enjoy some hot coffee sitting at the balcony while admiring the beauty of the place. Overnight at Gangtok.

    • DAY 6 : TRAVEL TO CHANGU LAKE, BABA MANDIR [1 HR 33 MIN (44.4 KM) VIA JAWAHARLAL NEHRU RD]

      As dawn breaks, casting warm rays through the window, it signals the start of another day filled with exploration and adventure. Begin your morning with a tranquil breakfast amidst the serene ambiance of the hotel surroundings. Before embarking on your journey, ensure to pack some dry foods for the day ahead.

      Your itinerary for the day promises an enriching experience, starting with a visit to Tsomgo Lake, also known as Changu Lake. Nestled 12,400 feet above sea level, this oval-shaped glacial lake sits just 40 kilometers from Gangtok City. Its Bhutia name, Tsomgo, translates to 'head of the lake,' reflecting its significance as the source of the lake. Encircled by snow-capped peaks, Tsomgo Lake's beauty is ever-changing, mirroring different hues with the shifting seasons. As you gaze upon its tranquil waters, immerse yourself in the sacred aura cherished by the locals.

      Next on your itinerary is the New Baba Harbhajan Singh Mandir, a memorial dedicated to the 'Hero of Nathula.' Located 12 kilometers from Tsomgo Lake and 52 kilometers from Gangtok City, this shrine commemorates the valiant soldier who served in the Indian Army. Baba Harbhajan Singh's tale is one of bravery and sacrifice, immortalized through his service and the shrine erected in his honor. As you pay homage to this revered figure, you'll be reminded of his unwavering dedication to his duty, even beyond the realms of mortality.

      After paying your respects, journey onwards to Nathula Pass, a historic mountain pass nestled in the East Sikkim district. At an elevation of 14,450 feet, this corridor once served as a vital passage between India and Tibet. Today, it offers a mesmerizing vista, granting glimpses of China across the border. Traverse the highest motorable roads, soaking in the breathtaking scenery of the Chumbi Valley below. Nathula Pass stands as a testament to the region's rich history and enduring significance.

      As you navigate through these awe-inspiring landscapes, relish the opportunity to witness nature's grandeur and the interwoven tapestry of cultures along these ancient trade routes. Take in every moment, for each step is a testament to the spirit of exploration and discovery. Finally, return to Gangtok, where the memories of the day's adventures will linger, enriching your journey through this remarkable region.

    • DAY 7 : CHERISH YOUR MEMORIES

      In the morning you can stroll about in the Gangtok MG Marg. Do your shopping, Later on, check out and drive to NJP / Bagdogra for return journey, and cherish your holiday forever.
